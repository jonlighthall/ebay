My name is Dr. Jon Lighthall and I run the eBay store World eCycling.  By profession I am a nuclear physicist, but I occasionally sell things on eBay as a hobby.  However, hobby or not, every transaction is important to me and I will always guarantee your satisfaction.  But don't take my word for it, look at my feedback; I've received over 1,000 positive feedback reviews from individual buyers.  And now after over ten years on ebay, I am finally a Top-rated Seller!

I began selling textbooks on eBay in 2003.  Most of the books I sold were surplus from around campus.  As a result, I was able to sell all of my books at drastically reduced prices.  Although I no longer have a great supply of textbooks, I continue to be committed to offering my buyers the best deals I can afford.  That's why all of my auctions start out at a penny.
