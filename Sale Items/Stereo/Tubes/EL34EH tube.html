Electro-Harmonix EL34 Quartet

$64.99 MSRP

For sale, a used matched quartet of EL34 vacuum tubes from Electro-Harmonix. All of the tubes are labeled EL34, Made in Russia, and feature the Electro-Harmonix logo that identifies the tubes as EL34EH. The letters EH at the end of the tube name simply indicate that it is an Electro-Harmonix tube.

Three of the tubes have the date code 0712, indicating that the tubes were made in February 2012. The fourth tube has the date code 0803, indicated that the tube was made in February 2003.

The tubes are all part of a quad-matched set. All of the tubes were tested to have plate current (Ip) 22 mA and transconductance (Gm) 4.6 S. The vacuum was also specified for the set as a "hard" vacuum. A hard vacuum should provide a longer lifetime and a higher distortion threshold.

Notes on This Auction:

I bought these tubes for the power stage of my hi-fi stereo amplifier in September 2012. They have been used approximately 1,500 hours since. The manufacturer of the amplifier advises that the power tubes should last 3,000 hours. Therefore, these tubes are thoroughly broken in and about half-way through their useful life.

I had these tubes installed when I accidentally shorted out the bias supply on one of the power tubes sockets in May 2014. However, the tubes still function, which is why I'm selling them. The tubes are labeled with marker on the base.
